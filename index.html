Our Recipies

Our Healthy diet recipes are made by choosing lean proteins, whole grains, plenty of fruits and vegetables, and minimizing added sugars and unhealthy fats.

Avocado Toast

Mash ripe avocado onto whole grain toast, sprinkle with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes for a quick and nutritious breakfast or snack.

Grilled Chicken Salad

Grill chicken breast and serve on a bed of mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, sliced cucumbers, and a squeeze of lemon for a protein-packed and satisfying salad.

Salmon and Asparagus Foil Pack

Place salmon fillet and asparagus spears on a sheet of foil, drizzle with lemon juice, and season with herbs. Seal the foil and bake for an easy and nutritious dinner.
